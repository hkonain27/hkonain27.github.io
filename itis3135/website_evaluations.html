Website Evaluations

Good Website

The Forever 21 website features a modern, user-friendly design that appeals to the audience.

  • Contrast: They effectively used vibrant colors to highlight important elements.
  • Repetition: The fonts, colors and styles are consistent and create a cohesive brand identity.
  • Alignment: The elements are well aligned and create a clear visual hierarchy.
  • Proximity: Similar items are grouped together that enhances the readability.

Overall, the Forever 21 website properly applies the CRAP principles, creating a visually appealing experience for users.

Bad Website

The Craigslist website has an outdated design that lacks modern aesthetics and user experience considerations.

  • Contrast: It has poor contrats between the text and background color making it hard to read the content.
  • Repetition: The inconsistent use of fonts and styles distract the audience from the cohesive look of the website.
  • Alignment: The elements are not well aligned and look chaotic.
  • Proximity: Similar items are not properly grouped together that makes it difficult to understand the content.

Overall, Craigslist fails to apply the CRAP principles, leading to a visually unappealing user experience.
