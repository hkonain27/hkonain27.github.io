About Me

About me:

My name is Hafsa Konain, junior at UNC, Charlotte. Since third grade, I've loved doing arts and crafts as a hobby. Making things by hand has evolved into a visual outlet for my ideas and thoughts over the years. Apart from doing Arts and craft, playing badminton, baking and photography are my other hobbies.

My Backgound in Arts and Craft:

Since a young age, I dedicated myself to arts and crafts. I have experimented with a variety of crafts since third grade, when my love for creating first emerged. Creating elaborate designs that showcase cultural artistry is something I enjoy doing in addition to drawing, painting, and paper crafts like origami and card crafting. I also participate in do it yourself activities like modelling clay, creating jewellery, and reusing existing objects into new decorations. More than simply a hobby, arts and crafts provide me with a means of relaxation, creative expression, and knowledge acquisition with every activity I take on.
