About Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code at UNC Charlotte is dedicated to empowering female students in computer science through skill development, networking, and creating a supportive community. Our mission is to inspire and support the next generation of women in tech.

By participating in Girls Who Code, members gain:

  • Technical Skills
  • Professional Networking Opportunities
  • Confidence in Problem Solving
