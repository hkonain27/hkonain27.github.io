GWC Events on Hold Until Next Semester

Dear GWC members and friends, As the current semester comes to an end, we would like to inform you that **Girls Who Code** at UNCC will not be hosting any events until the start of the next semester (January).

This semester has been truly exciting, filled with numerous workshops, hackathons, coding challenges, and networking opportunities. We were thrilled to see so many passionate members join us, and together we celebrated women in tech and empowered each other to grow as programmers and leaders.

As we take a short break, we encourage all our members to relax, recharge, and focus on their academic goals. The GWC executive team will be busy planning for the next semester, where we’ll continue to foster a welcoming community, bring in more hands-on coding experiences, and support our members in achieving their dreams in tech.

Stay tuned for updates and announcements on our website and social media channels. We can’t wait to welcome you back and continue building a strong network of young women in tech!

See you next semester!
