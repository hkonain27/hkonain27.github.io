How to Join Girls Who Code?

  • Membership is free for all UNC Charlotte students with a valid Student-ID number.

  • Become an official member of Girls Who Code by clicking here and completing the form.

Membership Eligibility:

  • Open to all students currently enrolled at UNC Charlotte, regardless of their major or technical background.
  • Non-student members, such as alumni, faculty, and staff, are welcome to participate in certain events and initiatives.

Membership Benefits:

  • Learn and improve technical skills through coding workshops and hackathons.
  • Network with industry professionals and peers passionate about technology.
  • Participate in leadership and volunteering opportunities within GWC's executive team and committees.
  • Be part of a supportive community aimed at empowering women in technology.
